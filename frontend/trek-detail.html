Overview

K2 or Mount Godwin-Austen, also known as Chogho Ree among the locals. The world's second-highest peak with a height of 8611m from the sea level. A perfect adventure for mountaineers and climbers.

But, before thinking about K2, it is highly recommended that an adventurous trip up to the K2 base camp must be made. It will help you understand what difficulties you may face if you make your determination towards K2. Trekking up to the K2 base camp gives a great experience of trekking in the extreme weather conditions and deadly routes of the Karakoram range. And you can unearth this experience with us.

The base camp is located at about 5100m in height from the sea level. The duration of this trekking usually ranges from two to three weeks. The base camp is not the highest point. You have to travel through more heights before reaching the base camp.

The journey usually begins from the village of Askole Shigar. Till Askoli, it is accessible by road. The road goes along with the Braldu River and goes through scenic landscapes, enchanting meadows, dense forests, and rocky terrains. This route covers Paju, an aesthetic campsite with breathtaking views of Paju Peak; Urdukas, A panoramic location enclosed by sky-high peaks and stunning views; Concordia, an immense glacial junction from where you can have spectacular views of K2, broad peak and other surrounding peaks; Baltoro Glacier and K2 base camp and then again to Islamabad following the same route.

Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival at Islamabad:

After the member arrives in Islamabad from their places, they will be transferred to the hotel and allowed to rest and prepare for further travel on the next day.

Day 02: Islamabad to Skardu:

In the morning flight from Islamabad to Skardu, we will leave. From the flight, we will reach Skardu by noon. In case of cancellation of a flight due to weather conditions or any other reason, we have arrangements to drive by road. We will reach Skardu in the next two days in case of travel by road. Our itinerary will be one day ahead if this case.

Day 03: Free day in Skardu for sightseeing:

A day free for sightseeing in Skardu. We will visit some nearby places in Skardu and will have a glimpse of the beauty of Skardu.

Day 4: Skardu to Askoli:

The journey goes alongside the Braldu River in the Shigar Valley. Then, we will start walking towards Askole.

Day 5: Trekking from Askoli to Jhola:

Start trekking towards Jhola. We have to cross the river Braldu by bridge. Jhola is also located near the Braldu River.

Day 6: Exploring Paju:

A break day for exploring the Paju peak base camp. The team makes the preparations during the day.

Day 7: Trek from Paju to Liligo:

Trekking to Liligo starting from the morning, involves beautiful panoramas and landscapes throughout the trek. It also includes a river crossing midway.

Day 8: Trek from Liligo to Urdukas:

Day 8 is going to be a tough trekking day as we have to climb and descend the glacial moraine. After crossing the Trango Tower and Uli Biaho, we will reach Urdukas and camp for the night.

Day 9: Trek from Urdukas to Goro II:

Trekking continues again towards Goro II. This time we will be trekking through the glacier. The view of Masherbrum and Muztagh Tower is visible from Goro II.

Day 10: Reaching Concordia:

We will reach Concordia by day 10. From Concordia, we can have a beautiful view of high snowcapped peaks such as Broad Peak, Mitre Peak, Gasherbrum and Sia Kangri as well as the aesthetic view of the Great K2.

Day 11: Reaching and camping at K2 Base Camp:

Trekking day towards K2 from Concordia. Tonight, we will spend at the base camp of K2.

Day 12: Returning to Concordia:

After spending a night at K2 Base Camp, our journey moves back to Concordia.

Day 13: Exploring Ali Camp:

Instead of following the previous route, we will take a slightly different route and move towards Ali camp. We will camp at Ali camp and here, the instructions will be given for crossing Gondogoro La.

Day 14: A day at Gondogoro La Base:

We will spend this day at the Gondogoro La base, and prepare to cross the challenging Gondogoro La crossing.

Day 15: Ascending to Gondogoro la:

Starting early in the morning, we will climb up the Gondogoro La. All members will be roped together. The views of K2, G1, Broad Peak, and G2 can be taken in the Baltoro region.

Day 16: Descending from Gondogoro La:

Descending from the Gondogoro pass needs more caution. Using fixed ropes and crampons, we will be coming down from rocky terrain, but the trek becomes easier as we move forward. By reaching Khuspang we will set our camp.

Day 17: Trekking to Hushe Valley:

As we descend, the rocky terrain becomes lush green landscapes. By reaching Hushe Valley, we will set up our camp at Saicho.

Day 18: Hushe to Skardu:

Early in the morning, we will be coming back to Skardu by using the Jeep service.

Day 19: Skardu to Islamabad:

The flight typically occurs in the morning. So we will reach Islamabad by noon on day 19. After reaching Islamabad, you are allowed to take a rest in the hotel.

Day 20: A free day at Islamabad:

In case of any flight cancellation due to weather issues or any other issue, we will reach Islamabad this day. Otherwise, we can utilize this day to explore Islamabad. We will be having a farewell dinner in Skardu or Islamabad as well.

Day 21: Towards own destination:

All members are free to go to their places. Our staff will transfer you all to the airport.
